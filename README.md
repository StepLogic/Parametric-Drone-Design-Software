# DPS
<h1>Drone preliminary Design Software</h1>
<p>Naming this was very difficult, I did not know what to call it .If you have better suggestion please share to my mail. I designed this software to try and help my colleagues breeze through the concept design phase fixed drone design while its I still under development, I thought of sharing it here to seek contributors and people to go Ove my work and help make it better. This project uses Aerosandbox from Peter Sharpe and tiGL for the 3D Modelling
It is capable of the following:
<ul>
  <li>Estimating  Mass moments of inertia</li>
 <li>	Estimating  various aerodynamic coefficients and stability derivatives</li> 
 <li>	Modeling and Export .obj and .stl model of drone design</li>
 <li>Estimating Center of Gravity</li>
 <li>Generate plots of various aerodynamic coefficients and stability derivatives</li> 
  </ul>
